# Splash Page for Meraki

The Splash Page for Meraki is a Laravel-based web application that serves as a gateway for providing complimentary internet access to users at the Baghdad International Airport. The web portal aims to enhance customer experience and satisfaction by offering free WiFi connectivity without any charges. 

## Key Features

- **Complimentary Internet Access**: The web portal allows passengers and visitors to connect to the WiFi network at the Baghdad International Airport without incurring any charges.

- **1 Hour Free Access**: Users are eligible for 1 hour of internet access for every 24 hours. After 24 hours, they can reconnect and enjoy another hour of free internet access.

- **User-Friendly Interface**: The Splash Page for Meraki has a user-friendly and intuitive interface, making it easy for users to connect to the network. Simple instructions are provided on the portal to guide users through the connection process.

- **Multi-Language Support**: Users can select their preferred language from the available options, ensuring a personalized experience when navigating the portal.

- **Cross-Device Compatibility**: The web portal is designed to work seamlessly across various devices, including laptops, tablets, and smartphones. Users can connect to the internet using their preferred devices.

- **Enhanced Security**: The Splash Page for Meraki prioritizes user security. It employs advanced security measures such as encryption and firewalls to protect users from potential cyber threats like hacking and phishing. Users can browse the internet with peace of mind, knowing their personal information is safeguarded.
